Scuba Diving Overview

Scuba diving allows people to travel to new depths and breathe underwater for extended periods of time. It creates an immersive experience for the diver, enabling deeper marine exploration, underwater construction, etc. There are different levels of certification in scuba diving that allow you to achieve new things within it. Each certification requires training and a test, including being able to scuba dive in the first place.
