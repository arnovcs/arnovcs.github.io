Our Purpose

Our purpose is to help Mother Ocean heal while raising awareness of varied Ecological Diving and providing disabled divers the access needed to use Scuba Diving as a means of physical and mental therapy, for their injuries and/or ailments.


How do we do this?


Fundraising + Connections

We organize various fundraising events that collectively help fund local ecological and humanitarian Scuba diving efforts throughout. Depending on the amount we collect, our reach grows as weve partnered up with varied dive charters and marine research institutions that host these types of dives. Our sole intention is to get “more fins in the water” and further these causes, increase their impact, and raise awareness.

Lasting Impressions

Imagine the impact on our ecosystems if every diver were to experience these unique, once-in-a-lifetime ecological and/or humanitarian diving experiences globally, at least once! We assure you that once you join one of these diving excursions, you won’t see recreational dives the same way again. It happened to us, and we’ve never looked back.


About
