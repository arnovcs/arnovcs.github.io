We need YOUR support

We wish it were as easy as waving a magic wand and making our oceans clean and healthy. Unfortunately, the reality is that organizing ecological-friendly events like ours comes at a significant cost. That’s why we’re turning to our community for help. We can’t do this alone, and we need your support to continue our mission to heal and protect Mother Ocean. No donation is too small – every little bit helps us cover the costs of putting on these vital events.

About


We’re a community of Aquatic Super Heroes, inspired by a collective passion for cleaning up, restoring damaged or decaying coral, protecting and preserving our Mother Ocean, beaches, reefs, and native habitats.
