Scuba Diving Overview

Scuba diving allows people to travel to new depths and breathe underwater for extended periods of time. It creates an immersive experience for the diver, enabling deeper marine exploration, underwater construction, etc. If you learn how, you will gain access to a whole new world.

There are different levels of certification in scuba diving that allow you to perform different things and push different limits in scuba diving. Each certification requires training and a test, including the certification for being able to scuba dive in the first place.
